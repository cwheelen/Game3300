Braised Salmon and Roasted Veggies

Here's the description:

Indulge in a wholesome and flavorful meal with Braised Salmon and Roasted Veggies. This dish combines the succulence of braised salmon with the earthy goodness of perfectly roasted vegetables, creating a symphony of tastes and textures that will delight your taste buds.


Uploaded by Cooking Together


Ingredients:
  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 1 pound baby potatoes, halved
  • 1 bunch asparagus, trimmed
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Steps:
  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Season the salmon fillets with salt, black pepper, and paprika.
  3. In an oven-safe skillet, heat olive oil over medium-high heat. Sear the salmon fillets skin-side down until golden brown. Flip the fillets and add minced garlic, lemon slices, and dried thyme to the skillet.
  4. Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and bake for about 12-15 minutes or until the salmon is cooked through.
  5. While the salmon is baking, toss halved baby potatoes, trimmed asparagus, and cherry tomatoes with olive oil, salt, and pepper on a baking sheet. Roast in the oven for 20-25 minutes or until the vegetables are tender and slightly crispy.
  6. Once the salmon is done, serve it over the roasted veggies. Garnish with fresh parsley and lemon wedges.
  7. Enjoy your delicious Braised Salmon and Roasted Veggies!
