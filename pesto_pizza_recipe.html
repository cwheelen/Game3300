Thin Crust Pesto Pizza

Here's the description:

Experience the delightful combination of a crispy thin crust and the vibrant flavors of fresh pesto with Thin Crust Pesto Pizza. This pizza is a celebration of simplicity and taste, perfect for those who appreciate the essence of classic Italian cuisine.


Uploaded by Cooking Together


Ingredients:
  • 1 pre-made thin pizza crust
  • 1/2 cup basil pesto sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1/4 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup black olives, sliced
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish
  • Crushed red pepper flakes (optional, for extra heat)
  • Extra virgin olive oil (for drizzling)

Steps:
  1. Preheat your oven to the temperature recommended for the pizza crust.
  2. Place the pre-made thin pizza crust on a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  3. Spread an even layer of basil pesto sauce over the pizza crust, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust.
  4. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the pesto.
  5. Top the pizza with halved cherry tomatoes and sliced black olives.
  6. Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese over the toppings for an extra burst of flavor.
  7. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Remove the pizza from the oven, garnish with fresh basil leaves, and optionally add crushed red pepper flakes for a spicy kick.
  9. Drizzle extra virgin olive oil over the finished pizza before slicing and serving.
  10. Enjoy your Thin Crust Pesto Pizza, a perfect blend of simplicity and flavor!
