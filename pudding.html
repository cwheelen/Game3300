Purple Yam Pudding

Here's the description:

Indulge in the rich and vibrant flavors of Purple Yam Pudding. This delightful dessert features a luscious blend of mashed purple yams, creating a naturally vibrant hue. Combined with a velvety pudding texture, each spoonful is a harmonious balance of sweetness and earthy goodness. The subtle hint of vanilla and the creamy essence of evaporated milk make this pudding a comforting treat. Perfect for those who appreciate unique flavors and a touch of exotic elegance in their desserts.


Uploaded by Cooking Together


Ingredients:
  • 3 purple yams
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tbsp melted butter
  • 1 cup rice flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups evaporated milk
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking powder

Steps:
  1. Peel the yams then boil in water until tender, about 30 min.
  2. Set aside to cool then grate the yams.
  3. Brush a 9x5x3in loaf pan with butter.
  4. Combine the grated yam, rice flour, sugar, evaporated milk, vanilla extract, and baking powder and then stir to mix thoroughly.
  5. Pour mixture into prepared loaf pan and steam for 40 min or until firm.
  6. Remove from the heat and set aside to cool for several minutes before serving.
