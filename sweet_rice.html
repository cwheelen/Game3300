Sweet Rice Square

Here's the description:

Indulge in the rich and vibrant flavors of Purple Yam Pudding. This delightful dessert features a luscious blend of mashed purple yams, creating a naturally vibrant hue. Combined with a velvety pudding texture, each spoonful is a harmonious balance of sweetness and earthy goodness. The subtle hint of vanilla and the creamy essence of evaporated milk make this pudding a comforting treat. Perfect for those who appreciate unique flavors and a touch of exotic elegance in their desserts.


Uploaded by Cooking Together


Ingredients:
  • 2 cups uncooked glutinous rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 cups thick coconut milk
  • 2.5 cups dark brown sugar or shaved palm sugar

Steps:
  1. Put rice and water in a rice cooker and cook until rice is done. Put the coconut milk in a casserole dish or pot and bring to the boil over medium heat.
  2. Add the brown sugar or palm sugar and simmer, stirring constantly until mixture becomes thick, about 15 minutes.
  3. Add the cooked rice and simmer, stirring constantly until most of the liquid has been absorbed. The mixture should still be slightly moist. Press into a pan and serve.
