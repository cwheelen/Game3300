Kimchi Pancake

Vegetarian
Here's the description:

Experience the bold and savory flavors of Korean cuisine with our Kimchi Pancake. This crispy, golden delight is a perfect marriage of tangy kimchi, green onions, and a savory pancake batter, creating a tantalizing dish that's both appetizing and comforting.


Uploaded by Cooking Together


Ingredients:
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 cup kimchi, chopped
  • 2 green onions, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup kimchi juice
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Steps:
  1. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, water, and beaten egg until you achieve a smooth batter.
  2. Add chopped kimchi, sliced green onions, kimchi juice, soy sauce, sesame oil, and salt to the batter. Mix well to combine.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a non-stick skillet over medium heat. Ladle the batter into the skillet, spreading it evenly to create a pancake.
  4. Cook until the edges are crispy and golden, then flip the pancake and cook the other side until fully cooked and crispy.
  5. Repeat the process with the remaining batter, adding more oil if needed. Once cooked, transfer the pancakes to a plate lined with paper towels to absorb excess oil.
  6. Serve hot, sliced into wedges, and enjoy the delightful fusion of flavors in our Kimchi Pancake!
