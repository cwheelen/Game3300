Beef Pho

Lactose Free
Here's the description:

Embark on a culinary journey with our flavorful Beef Pho, a Vietnamese classic that combines aromatic spices, tender beef, and rice noodles in a comforting broth. This heartwarming bowl of pho is a harmonious blend of savory and aromatic elements that will transport you to the bustling streets of Hanoi.


Uploaded by Cooking Together


Ingredients:
  • 1 pound beef brisket, thinly sliced
  • 8 cups beef broth
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 3-inch piece of ginger, sliced
  • 3-4 star anise
  • 3-4 whole cloves
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1 tablespoon coriander seeds
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 8 oz rice noodles, cooked according to package instructions
  • Fresh bean sprouts, basil, lime wedges, and sliced chili for garnish

Steps:
  1. In a large pot, char the onion and ginger over an open flame or under a broiler for a few minutes until fragrant.
  2. Add the beef brisket, beef broth, star anise, cloves, cinnamon stick, coriander seeds, fish sauce, soy sauce, and sugar to the pot. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for at least 1 hour.
  3. Strain the broth, discarding the solids, and return the clear broth to the pot. Adjust seasoning if needed.
  4. Cook rice noodles according to package instructions and divide them among serving bowls.
  5. Ladle the hot broth over the noodles and top with sliced beef brisket.
  6. Garnish with bean sprouts, basil, lime wedges, and sliced chili. Serve hot and savor the authentic flavors of our Beef Pho!
