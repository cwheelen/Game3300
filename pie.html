Saskatoon Berry Pie

Vegetarian
Here's the description:

Delight your taste buds with the wholesome sweetness of our Saskatoon Berry Pie. Bursting with juicy Saskatoon berries encased in a flaky, golden crust, this classic pie is a celebration of summer flavors that will leave you craving more.


Uploaded by Cooking Together


Ingredients:
  • 4 cups fresh Saskatoon berries
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, cut into small pieces
  • 2 refrigerated pie crusts (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)
  • Granulated sugar (for sprinkling)

Steps:
  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a large bowl, gently toss together Saskatoon berries, granulated sugar, flour, lemon juice, and vanilla extract until the berries are evenly coated.
  3. Line a pie dish with one of the pie crusts, leaving any excess hanging over the edges. Pour the berry mixture into the crust, and dot the top with small pieces of butter.
  4. Place the second pie crust over the berries, sealing the edges with the bottom crust. Trim any excess dough and crimp the edges to seal.
  5. Brush the top crust with beaten egg and sprinkle with granulated sugar for a golden finish.
  6. Make a few small slits in the top crust to allow steam to escape. Bake for 45-50 minutes or until the crust is golden and the filling is bubbly.
  7. Allow the pie to cool before slicing. Serve and savor the delightful taste of our Saskatoon Berry Pie!
