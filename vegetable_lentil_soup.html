Vegetable Lentil Soup

Here's the description:

Warm up with our hearty Vegetable Lentil Soup, a wholesome blend of nutritious vegetables and protein-packed lentils. This soul-soothing soup is a delightful combination of flavors, perfect for a comforting meal that nourishes both body and spirit.


Uploaded by Cooking Together


Ingredients:
  • 1 cup dry lentils, rinsed
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Steps:
  1. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add diced onion, carrots, and celery. Sauté until vegetables are softened.
  2. Add minced garlic, ground cumin, paprika, and turmeric. Stir to combine and sauté for an additional 2 minutes.
  3. Rinse lentils and add them to the pot along with vegetable broth and diced tomatoes. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 20-25 minutes or until lentils are tender.
  5. Adjust seasoning as needed and serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley. Enjoy the wholesome goodness of our Vegetable Lentil Soup!
