Marinated Steak with Sauteed Asparagus

Here's the description:

Indulge in the savory delight of Marinated Steak with Sauteed Asparagus. This recipe combines the bold flavors of perfectly marinated steak with the crisp freshness of sauteed asparagus. A symphony of tastes that will elevate your dining experience.


Uploaded by Cooking Together


Ingredients:
  • 1 lb steak (your preferred cut)
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 bunch fresh asparagus, trimmed
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Steps:
  1. Start by marinating the steak: In a bowl, mix soy sauce, minced garlic, olive oil, Worcestershire sauce, Dijon mustard, salt, and black pepper. Coat the steak with the marinade and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Preheat your grill or pan. Cook the marinated steak to your desired level of doneness.
  3. While the steak is cooking, heat a separate pan over medium heat. Add butter and saute the trimmed asparagus until they are tender-crisp.
  4. Drizzle lemon juice over the sauteed asparagus for a fresh flavor boost.
  5. Serve the cooked steak on a plate, accompanied by the sauteed asparagus. Enjoy your Marinated Steak with Sauteed Asparagus!
